MySpot

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

MySpot is designed with your privacy in mind. We don't collect personal data, don't require an account, and everything you save stays on your device. Here's the full picture.

Overview

MySpot ("we," "our," or "the app") is an iOS application that helps you save and find your parking location. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it's used, and your rights as a user.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. MySpot does not require you to create an account, and your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up via iCloud.

Information We Collect

MySpot collects only the information needed to provide its core features. All data is stored locally on your device.

Location Data

When you save a parking spot, MySpot accesses your device's GPS to record the coordinates of your location. This data is stored on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.

Photos

You may optionally add photos of your parking spot or vehicle. These photos are stored locally on your device. MySpot does not upload photos to any server or third-party service.

Vehicle Information

If you choose to add vehicles, MySpot stores details you provide — such as vehicle name, make, model, color, and license plate — locally on your device.

Notes & Preferences

Any notes, timer settings, notification preferences, and app settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage mechanisms.

Parking History

Premium users may access a history of past parking sessions, including timestamps, coordinates, and associated notes or photos. This data is stored locally on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

MySpot does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identifying information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account credentials of any kind
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Analytics or usage data
  • Crash reports sent to our servers
  • Location data in the background or when the app is not in use

How Your Data Is Used

Data collected by MySpot is used solely to provide the app's features:

  • Displaying your saved parking location on a map
  • Navigating you back to your parking spot
  • Sending local timer notifications you have configured
  • Managing your vehicle profiles
  • Providing smart timer suggestions based on your parking history (Premium)

Your data is never used for advertising, profiling, or shared with third parties for any purpose.

Third-Party Services

MySpot uses the following Apple system services:

  • Apple Maps / MapKit — for displaying maps and providing navigation. Subject to Apple's privacy policy.
  • Apple StoreKit — for processing the optional one-time Premium upgrade ($3.99). Payment is handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information.
  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) — for delivering local parking timer notifications. These are scheduled on-device and do not pass through our servers.

MySpot does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

iCloud & Data Backup

MySpot stores all data locally on your device using Apple's standard storage. If you have iCloud backup enabled on your device, your app data may be included in your device backup as part of Apple's standard backup process. This backup is managed entirely by Apple and governed by Apple's iCloud privacy policy. MySpot does not independently sync or upload data to iCloud or any other cloud service.

Permissions

MySpot may request the following device permissions:

  • Location (While Using App) — required to save your parking coordinates and display your position on the map. Location is only accessed when you are actively using the app.
  • Camera / Photo Library — optional, used only if you choose to add photos to a parking session or vehicle profile.
  • Notifications — optional, used to deliver parking timer alerts you configure within the app.

You can manage or revoke any of these permissions at any time in your device's Settings app.

Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored on your device and under your control. You can delete individual parking sessions, vehicles, or your full parking history at any time within the app. Uninstalling MySpot will remove all locally stored app data from your device.

Children's Privacy

MySpot is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
